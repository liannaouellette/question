Avocado Toast with Eggs








INGREDIENTS

2 slices of white bread

1 ripe avocado

2 large eggs

1 tablespoon olive oil or butter

Salt and pepper to taste














Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5 minutes     5 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
10 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Cut the ripe avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into
a bowl. Mash the avocado using a fork until smooth. Season with salt,
pepper, and additional spices if desired.

Step 2

Toast the slices of white bread to your preferred level of doneness.

Step 3

Fill a pot with water and bring it to a gentle simmer (not boiling).
Crack the eggs into separate small bowls or cups. Create a gentle
whirlpool in the simmering water and carefully slide in the eggs one
at a time.

Step 4

Cook for about 3-4 minutes until the whites are set but the yolks are
still runny. Remove the poached eggs with a slotted spoon and place them
on a paper towel to drain excess water.

Step 5

Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto the toasted bread slices. Place
the poached eggs on top of the avocado spread.
