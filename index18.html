Bread with Peanut Butter and Bananas













INGREDIENTS

2 slices of white bread


4 tablespoons peanut butter (unsweetened)


1 medium-sized banana, sliced












Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-10 minutes      N/A
Total Time:      Servings:
20-25 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Toast 2 slices of white bread until they reach your desired level of toasted.

Step 2

Slice the banana into thin rounds.

Step 3

Spread 4 tablespoon of peanut butter evenly on each slice of toasted white bread.

Step 4

Arrange the banana slices on top of one slice of bread with peanut butter.

Step 5

Place the other slice of bread with peanut butter on top to form a sandwich.
