Lentil Stew








INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup dried lentils, rinsed and drained

1 medium carrot, chopped

1/2 onion, chopped

1 stalk celery, chopped

1/2 cup cooked beans

1 cup vegetable broth or water

2 tablespoons olive oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 teaspoon cumin

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     30-40 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
45-60 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Add onions, carrots, and
celery. Saut for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.

Step 2

Add minced garlic and cumin, and saut for an additional minute
until fragrant.

Step 3

Stir in the rinsed lentils, cooked beans, and vegetable broth or water.
Bring to a boil.

Step 4

Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for about 25-30 minutes or until the
lentils are tender.

Step 5

Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
