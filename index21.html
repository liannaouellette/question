Greek Yogurt With Honey, Granola And Berries








INGREDIENTS

1 cup of Greek yogurt (unsweetened)

1 cup assorted seasonal berries

2 tablespoons of honey

Chopped nuts or granola














Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-10 minutes       N/A
Total Time:      Servings:
5-10 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Prepare Ingredients:

Wash the seasonal berries thoroughly and pat them dry with a paper towel.

Step 2

Assemble the Dish:

Place the Greek yogurt in a serving bowl.

Top the Greek yogurt with a generous portion of assorted seasonal berries.

Drizzle honey and add granola or diced nuts over the pears and yogurt.
