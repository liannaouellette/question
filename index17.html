Roasted Chicken with Brussels Sprouts and Sweet Potatoes














INGREDIENTS

1 bone-in, skin-on chicken thigh or breast

1 cup Brussels sprouts, halved or quartered

1 medium-sized sweet potato, peeled and diced

2 tablespoons olive oil or melted butter

Optional: 1-2 tablespoons chopped nuts for extra calories















Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     45-60 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
60-80 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 400F (200C).

Step 2

Place the chicken, Brussels sprouts, and diced sweet potatoes on a
baking sheet. Drizzle olive oil or melted butter over the chicken and
vegetables. Season everything with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and
pepper. Toss to coat evenly.

Step 3

Roast in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until the chicken
is cooked through (internal temperature reaches 165F or 74C) and the
vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized.

Step 4

If using nuts for extra calories, sprinkle chopped nuts over the
roasted vegetables before serving.
