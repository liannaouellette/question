Whole Grain Wrap








INGREDIENTS

1 large whole grain wrap or tortilla

3-4 tablespoons hummus

5-6 slices of turkey breast

1 cup roasted vegetables

1 cup fresh greens

1 tablespoon olive oil

Optional: Avocado slices or shredded cheese











Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-10 minutes     10-20 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
15-30 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 400F (200C).

Step 2

Toss the vegetables in olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Roast
in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until tender and
slightly caramelized.

Step 3

Lay the whole grain wrap or tortilla on a clean surface. Spread the
hummus evenly over the wrap. Layer the turkey slices on top of the hummus.
Add the roasted vegetables and fresh greens on top of the turkey. Optionally,
include avocado slices or shredded cheese for extra calories and flavor.

Step 4

Fold in the sides of the wrap and roll it tightly, starting from the
bottom, to form a tight wrap. Optional: Grilling

Step 5

To enhance flavors, you can grill the assembled wrap on a skillet or grill
pan for a few minutes until it's lightly toasted and crispy.
