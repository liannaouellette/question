Butternut Squash Soup With White Bread













INGREDIENTS

1 cup diced butternut squash

1/2 small onion, diced

1 garlic clove, minced

1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Salt and pepper to taste

1 tablespoon olive oil

1/2 cup heavy cream

Optional garnish: fresh herbs or a dollop of sour cream

2 slices of white breadr







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     30-40 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
45-60 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Add diced onion and garlic,
saut until translucent.

Step 2

Add diced butternut squash, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Cook for about
5 minutes.

Step 3

Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat
and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the squash is tender.

Step 4

Use an immersion blender or transfer the mixture to a blender to pure
until smooth.

Step 5

Adjust seasoning if needed.

Step 6

Stir in the heavy cream. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.

Step 5

Serve hot and garnish with fresh herbs or a dollop of sour cream if desired.

Step 5

Toast 2 slices of white bread until lightly browned.
