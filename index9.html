Pumpkin Pancakes








INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup pumpkin puree

1/2 cup all-purpose flour or whole wheat flour

1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)

1 large egg

1 tablespoon brown sugar or maple syrup

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

Pinch of salt

Butter or oil for cooking







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-10 minutes     10-15 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
15-25 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

In a mixing bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, milk, egg,
and brown sugar until well combined.

Step 2

In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, cinnamon,
nutmeg, ginger, and salt.

Step 3

Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until just
combined. Be careful not to overmix; it's okay if the batter is a bit lumpy.

Step 4

Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly
grease it with butter or oil.

Step 5

Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook
until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden
brown on both sides.

Step 6

Repeat with the remaining batter.
