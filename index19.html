Quinoa Salad













INGREDIENTS

1 cup cooked quinoa

1 small beetroot, peeled and grated or thinly sliced (raw)

1 tablespoon chopped walnuts

1 cup fresh spinach leaves

1 tablespoon chopped cashews

Dressing: Olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste











Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-15 minutes     15-20 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
25-35 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Cook quinoa according to package instructions and let it cool. Peel
and grate the beetroot or thinly slice it (raw). Crumble the goat
cheese, chop the walnuts, and prepare the spinach leaves.

Step 2

In a bowl, combine the cooked and cooled quinoa, grated or thinly sliced
raw beetroot, crumbled goat cheese, chopped walnuts, fresh spinach
leaves, and chopped cashews.

Step 3

Drizzle the salad with olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt
and pepper according to taste. Toss the salad gently to combine all
the ingredients and coat them with the dressing.
