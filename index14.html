Baked Acorn Stuffed Squash








INGREDIENTS

1 small acorn squash

2/4 cup quinoa (uncooked)

2/4 pound ground beef

1 tablespoon olive oil

1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Salt and pepper to taste

Optional: Fresh herbs like parsley or thyme for garnish










Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-20 minutes     50-60 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
60-80 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preparation:

Preheat the oven to 375F (190C).

Cut the acorn squash in half lengthwise and scoop out the seeds.

Rub the inner sides of the squash halves with olive oil, sprinkle
with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Place them face down on a baking
sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until tender.

Step 2

Cooking the Filling:

While the squash is baking, cook the quinoa according to package instructions.
In a skillet over medium heat, add olive oil and brown the ground beef. Add
the seasonal vegetables and cook until softened.

Mix in the cooked quinoa and season the mixture with salt, pepper, or
additional spices to taste.

Step 3

Stuffing the Squash:

Once the squash halves are cooked and tender, remove them from the oven.

Fill each squash half with the quinoa, ground beef, and vegetable mixture.

Garnish with chopped cilantro if desired.
