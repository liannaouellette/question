Spinach, Apple and Chicken Salad with Walnuts












INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups fresh baby spinach

  • 2 cups arugula

  • 1 apple (e.g., Honeycrisp, Gala,
    Golden Delicious), thinly sliced

  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped

  • 1/4 cup goat cheese or
    blue cheese

  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced

  • 1/4 boneless, skinless
    chicken breasts

  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup

  • 150g can chickpea, drained and rinsed

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

  • naan bread, to serve

  • Salt and pepper to taste



Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     10-15 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
25-35 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 400F (200C).

Step 2

Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then place them on
a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes, or until cooked through.

Step 3

In a large bowl, combine the spinach, arugula, sliced apple, chopped
walnuts, and sliced red onion

Step 4

In a separate bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, maple syrup,
olive oil, and Dijon mustard to make the vinaigrette.

Step 5

Slice the cooked chicken breasts and add them to the salad.

Step 6

Drizzle the vinaigrette over the salad and toss to combine
