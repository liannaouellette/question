Apple Cinnamon Overnight Oats







INGREDIENTS

  • ½ cup old-fashioned rolled oats

  • ½ cup 2% milk

  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds (Optional)

  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup

  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon

  • Pinch of salt

  • 1 diced apple

  • 2 tablespoons toasted pecans (Optional)





Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-10 minutes       N/A
Total Time:      Servings:
10 minutes           1

Directions

Step 1

Combine oats, almond milk, chia seeds (if using), maple syrup, cinnamon
and salt in a pint-sized jar and stir. Cover and refrigerate overnight.


Step 2

Before serving, top with apple and pecans, if desired.
