Omelette With Spinach, Mushrooms, And Tomatoes








INGREDIENTS

2 eggs

1/2 cup fresh spinach leaves, chopped

1/4 cup mushrooms, sliced

1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

1 slice of orange (or cheddar) cheese

1 tablespoon olive oil or butter

Salt and pepper to taste










Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-10 minutes     5-10 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
10-20 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preparation:

Whisk the eggs in a bowl until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.

Chop the spinach leaves, slice the mushrooms, and halve the cherry tomatoes.

Tear or cut the slice of orange cheese into smaller pieces.

Step 2

Cooking:

Heat olive oil or butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

Add sliced mushrooms to the skillet and sauté for 2-3 minutes until
they begin to soften.

Add chopped spinach and halved cherry tomatoes to the skillet. Cook
for another 1-2 minutes until the spinach wilts slightly.

Step 3

Making the Omelette:

Place the torn/cut pieces of orange (or cheddar) cheese evenly over
the omelette.

Allow the eggs to set around the edges of the skillet. Gently lift the
edges of the omelette with a spatula to let the uncooked egg flow underneath.

Once the omelette is mostly set but still slightly runny on top, fold it
in half with the spatula.

Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the omelette is fully cooked
through and the cheese melts.

Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.
