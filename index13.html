Lentil Soup With Carrots, Onions, And Celery








INGREDIENTS

1 cup dried lentils

1 medium onion, diced

2 medium carrots, diced

2 stalks celery, diced

4 cups vegetable or chicken broth

2 tablespoons olive oil

2 garlic cloves, minced

1 teaspoon dried thyme

Salt and pepper to taste







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-15 minutes     40-60 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
50-75 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Prepare Ingredients:

Rinse the lentils under cold water and dice the onion, carrots, and celery.

Step 2

Cooking:

In a pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onion, carrots, and
celery. Cook for 5-7 minutes until softened.

Add minced garlic and dried thyme, cook for an additional minute.

Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth and add the rinsed lentils. Bring
to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for about 25-30 minutes or
until lentils are tender.

Season with salt and pepper to taste.
