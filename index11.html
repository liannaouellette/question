Chickpea Curry With Brown Rice








INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup cooked chickpeas (canned or pre-cooked)

1/2 cup cooked brown rice

1/2 cup cauliflower florets

1/2 onion, finely chopped

1 garlic clove, minced

1/2 tablespoon olive oil

1/2 cup canned diced tomatoes

1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

1/2 teaspoon ground coriander

1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

Salt to taste

Chopped cilantro for garnish (optional) m n n







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
20-30 minutes     25-35 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
45-65 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Prepare Ingredients:

Cook 1/2 cup of brown rice according to package instructions.

Steam or blanch the cauliflower florets until they are tender yet firm.
Set aside.

Step 2

Cook Chickpea Curry:

Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté
until translucent.

Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.

Stir in the diced tomatoes, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric
powder, chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5 minutes until the tomatoes
break down and the mixture thickens slightly.

Add the cooked chickpeas to the tomato mixture and simmer for an
additional 5-7 minutes until heated through.

Step 3

Assemble the Dish:

Serve the chickpea curry over the cooked brown rice.

Arrange the steamed cauliflower florets on the side or mix them
into the curry.

Garnish with chopped cilantro if desired.
