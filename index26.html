Baked Chicken Breast








INGREDIENTS

For the Baked Chicken: 1 boneless, skinless
chicken breast 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 teaspoon
paprika 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder Salt and pepper
to taste

For the Roasted Cauliflower: 1 cup cauliflower
florets 1 tablespoon olive oil 1/2 teaspoon smoked
paprika 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder Salt and pepper
to taste

For the Brown Rice: 1/2 cup cooked brown rice

For the Steamed Broccoli: 1 cup broccoli florets









Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-15 minutes     45-60 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
55-75 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 400F (200C). Rub the chicken breast with olive oil
and season it with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Place the
seasoned chicken breast on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes
until it's cooked through.

Step 2

Toss the cauliflower florets with olive oil, smoked paprika, cumin
powder, salt, and pepper. Spread them on a baking sheet and roast in
the oven alongside the chicken for about 20-25 minutes or until the
cauliflower is tender and lightly browned.

Step 3

Cook the brown rice according to package instructions.

Step 4

In a steamer basket over boiling water, steam the broccoli florets
for about 5-7 minutes until they are tender but still crisp.

Step 5

Place the cooked chicken breast on a plate. Serve it with roasted
cauliflower, a side of brown rice, and steamed broccoli
