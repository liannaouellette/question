Spinach, Feta and Cherry Tomato Omelet








INGREDIENTS

3 large eggs

1 cup fresh spinach leaves

1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)

1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

1 tablespoon olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Optional: 2 tablespoons heavy cream or whole milk
for added creaminess and calories







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-7 minutes     5-7 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
10-14 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Whisk the eggs in a bowl. If using optional heavy cream or milk, add
it to the eggs and mix well. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick skillet
over medium heat.

Step 2

Add the spinach leaves to the skillet and sauté for about 1-2 minutes
until wilted. Pour the whisked egg mixture evenly over the spinach.
Sprinkle the halved cherry tomatoes and crumbled feta cheese across the
egg mixture. Allow the omelette to cook undisturbed for a few minutes
until the edges start to set.

Step 3

Gently lift the edges of the omelette with a spatula and fold it in half.
Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the eggs are fully set and the
cheese is melted.

Step 4

Season the omelette with salt and pepper to taste. Slide the omelette
onto a plate and serve immediately.
