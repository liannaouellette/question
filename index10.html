Quinoa Salad With A Little Extra








INGREDIENTS

For Salad:

1 1/4 cup uncooked quinoa

1/2 medium-sized sweet potato, peeled and diced

1/2 tablespoon olive oil

Pinch of salt and pepper

1 tablespoon dried cranberries

1/2 tablespoon pumpkin seeds (pepitas)

1/2 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (optional)

For The Dressing:

3/4 teaspoon olive oil

1/2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar or lemon juice

1/4 teaspoon honey or maple syrup (optional)

Pinch of salt and pepper







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-15 minutes     15-20 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
25-35 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Prepare Quinoa: Rinse 1/4 cup of quinoa under cold water. In a small
saucepan, combine the quinoa with 1/2 cup of water. Bring to a boil,
then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the
water is absorbed. Fluff with a fork and let it cool.

Step 2

Roast Sweet Potato: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss diced sweet
potato with 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on
a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes or until tender and slightly
crispy. Let cool.

Step 3

Prepare Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together 3/4 teaspoon olive oil,
1/2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar or lemon juice, 1/4 teaspoon honey
or maple syrup (if using), salt, and pepper.

Step 4

Assemble Salad: In a bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, roasted sweet
potatoes, dried cranberries, pumpkin seeds, and chopped parsley (if
using). Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
